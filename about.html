History

The Maletsunyane Adventure Festival was created to promote tourism in Semonkong. The event celebrates local culture, food, music and outdoor adventure activities around the famous Maletsunyane Falls.

Significance

The festival supports local businesses, promotes Basotho culture and encourages international tourists to visit Lesotho.

Target Tourists

  • Adventure tourists
  • Cultural tourists
  • International visitors
  • Local families
